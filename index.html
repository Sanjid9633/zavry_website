About us

Who we are?

A vibrant Startup collective of young innovators from diverse worlds of Engineering and Commerce, we’ve united with a shared vision: to transform the restaurant industry with a fresh and powerful twist. What began as a close-knit circle of friends has blossomed into a team of eight passionate individuals (as of November 2024), each bringing unique strengths and boundless dedication. We’ve crossed college campuses, transcended fields of study, and broken through conventional boundaries—all for one purpose: to breathe life into Zavry. Vision

How we see our future

Our vision for Zavry is deeply rooted in our pride and passion for the food culture of our country. We see Zavry growing into more than just a platform; we want it to be a trusted space where restaurants and customers come together to celebrate and elevate local dining. We aim to build Zavry into a name people recognize for quality, transparency, and connection—a platform where the first step to becoming a celebrated eatery is simply registering with us. We envision Zavry as a trusted partner to both small family-owned diners and well-established restaurants, helping them reach people who genuinely appreciate great food. By making Zavry the go-to place for exploring and reviewing local eateries, we’re setting our sights on becoming a unicorn startup, one that’s not only successful but also meaningful and impactful. This journey is about more than growth; it's about building a platform that respects and nurtures our local dining community

Mission

How do we do and why?

Our mission at Zavry is to create a vibrant platform that connects restaurants and customers, fostering seamless communication and mutual trust. By offering restaurants a dedicated space to showcase and promote their unique offerings, we aim to be an essential partner in their growth. Zavry is designed to empower eateries with effective tools for reaching new audiences, all while providing customers with a trusted, engaging experience as they explore the best of local dining. Beyond our core platform, we’re committed to pursuing innovative projects within the restaurant industry. Through these initiatives, Zavry fills critical gaps and builds lasting trust with both customers and restaurant owners, ultimately becoming a key player in this sector. We are here to make local dining not just accessible but exceptional.
