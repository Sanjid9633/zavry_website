About us

Who we are?

A vibrant Startup collective of young innovators from diverse worlds of Engineering and Commerce, we’ve united with a shared vision: to transform the restaurant industry with a fresh and powerful twist. What began as a close-knit circle of friends has blossomed into a team of eight passionate individuals (as of November 2024), each bringing unique strengths and boundless dedication. We’ve crossed college campuses, transcended fields of study, and broken through conventional boundaries—all for one purpose: to breathe life into Zavry. With ambition in our hearts and a fierce commitment to excellence, we’re on a journey to build a new startup legacy. Together, we're pouring our best into this venture, driven by a belief in what Zavry can become and the impact it can have. This is just the beginning, and we’re ready to inspire, innovate, and redefine. we say "Zearch.Zavour.Zhare"

: Team ZAVRY
